Who are we?

Welcome to Faddy Duck Restaurant. We are a Beijing style deli. At Faddy Duck we are passionate about bringing the authentic flavours of Peking(Beijing) to Dublin, especially Peking Roasted Duck(北京烤鸭).

What is Peking Roasted Duck?

Peking roasted duck was a staple of imperial menus in China before the Ming Dynasty, and is today considered one of China's National dishes. The duck is glazed with a thick layer of syrup and hung to dry. Then roasted either in a closed oven or a hung oven.
